Biography

Jonás graduated in Physics from the University of Salamanca in 2012, finished a M.Sc. in Astrophysics at the Autonomous University of Madrid in 2013 (thesis: “Velocity fields around voids”), and carried out Ph.D. studies at Centro de Estudios de Física del Cosmos de Aragón (CEFCA) from 2013 to 2017 (thesis: “New techniques for an optimal cosmological exploitation of galaxy surveys”). From 2017 to 2020, he was a postdoctoral appointee in the Cosmological Physics and Advanced Computing (CPAC) group at Argonne National Laboratory. Since 2020, he is a postdoctoral appointee at the Donostia International Physics Center (DIPC).
